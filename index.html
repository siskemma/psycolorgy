psycolorgy

/sigh-kolor-GEE/ n. the concept more commonly known as the psychology of color.

Color is everywhere. Like opening a new box of crayons or watching the evening sunset, color impacts the way we feel and how experience life. This notion is called color psychology, which is the idea that specific color values induce psychological effects on us. Here, you will learn more about what it means to not only feel blue, but the rest of the spectrum too.
All of the factual information on this site was retrieved from colorpsychology.org. All images (besides the ones below) were retrieved from pexels.com. Any "I" statements are opinions from myself, Emma, the creator of this site. And personally, I think you're going to enjoy yourself here! To begin, select from any of the color links located at the top of the page.
